Frequently Asked Questions We understand you're going to have questions, therefore we have provided you with some answers to frequently asked questions.

Q.) What is remote support?

A.) Remote support is a new technology that allows experienced techs to connect to your computer and "control" it as if

that tech was sitting in front of your machine. This allows us to provide fast superior tech support to you at a reduced

cost due to no travel expenses.

Q.) Is Remote Support Safe? Can my tech gain access to my PC at a later time without my permission?

A.) Yes and No. Yes Remote Support is safe. Our technology is designed with your security at heart. Prior to your tech connecting

to your machine, your tech will provide you with a 9-digit Single-Use Support Key, that is only valid for one session.

Without you entering this support key on our website we cannot connect to your machine unless you activate Unattended Support™ on your account.

Furthermore, not only is that 9-digit Single-Use Support Key valid for one session, but is only valid for a limited time.

Q.) Now @gNosis provides Unattended Support™, what the heck does this mean? Is my PC and data still safe?

A.) Yes @gNosis now provides a new feature called Unattended Support™ which is now included in all of our unlimited plans.

What this means is now you as a member have greater flexibility to control your schedule and when you want your computer serviced.

By activating Unattended Support™ on your computer, this allows your tech to create a secret code which in turn will allow us to

connect to your computer at a later time (determined by you) without the need for you to be home.

So busy moms can be out car pooling and come home to a repaired machine.

Only your assigned agent will know this code, and you can temporarily revoke (disable) or even permanently revoke (disable)

Unattended Support™ at any time. Furthermore we adhere to a strict customer integrity and data security policy.

Our file transfer feature that allows us to send files back and forth to your computer is deactivated during unattended support sessions.

Q.) Is my personal data safe if I use Remote Support services?

A.) Yes. Obviously our service allows us to transfer data back and forth between our machine and yours, and allows us to install

software on your system, but all of this is done in front of you. We will never connect to your system without you being present in front of your machine.

Q.) What if I am not satisfied with my tech and no longer want to remain connected?

A.) Certainly we always want you to be satisfied, but if you are not, you can boot us from your system at anytime

with our easy to use software. Also, if you are ever unhappy with our service please report it to us by clicking here.

Q.) Will I have to download anything to my PC in order to use your services

A.) Yes, upon initial connection to your system you will be required to download our software.

This is a one time occurrence (per PC). Our software is safe, spy-ware free, and does not use any system resources when not in use.

Furthermore it is secure as we previously stated.
